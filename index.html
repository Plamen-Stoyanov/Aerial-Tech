What we do

Aerial Tech Solutions started with the vision of revolutionising the re-vegetation industry. We saw a need to deliver rapid re-forestation in disturbed and desertified land.
Our goal is to re-green the earth and disrupt the progress of climate change using the most up-to-date and cutting-edge technology.
As a not-for-profit organisation all surplus funds are reinvested into further research and development.

Efficiency

We have invested research into the most effective systems for large-scale re-planting. Our fleet of drones and expert pilots can replant at rates over 10 square kilometers per day.
This ensures all our clients the fastest results in their re-foresting projects.

Progress

We constantly update and develop all system components. Through frequent testing and research we stay at the cutting edge of remote flight systems and forest rehabilitation.
We pride ourselves on being global leaders in all aspects of drone technology.

Community

Every project we undertake places special emphasis on the people closest to the re-planting site. We work closely with all stakeholders to deliver the best benefits to the most people.
We service a diverse range of clients - from large corporations to rural farming communites.
